How to use this with school visits

  • Ask each school what weekly PE and after-school sport they run for Reception and Key Stage 1.
  • Check if wraparound care can connect with local clubs/swim lessons.
  • Use home-to-school travel time plus one nearby community facility as your baseline routine test.

Admission Timing

Reception usually starts in September after a child turns 4. For a child aged 2 on 28 February 2026, likely entry points are September 2027 or September 2028, depending on date of birth.
